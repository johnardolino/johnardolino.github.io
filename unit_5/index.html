Welcome to Dad Wise! This is one father's version of what it's like to be a stay-at-home-parent and homemaker. It's hard out there for a full-time parent, the responsibility of keeping someone alive every day is exhausting, while at the same time completely and utterly rewarding. With the cunning use of humor and creativity, Dad Wise hopes to bring some laughter to one of the hardest jobs in the world.

Adventure awaits!
